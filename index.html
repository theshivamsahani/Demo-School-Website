About Shivam Public School

Shivam Public School, established in 2009, stands as a beacon of holistic education and character building. Our vision is rooted in fostering not only academic brilliance but also values, critical thinking, and leadership in every student.

Our vibrant campus, cutting-edge labs, digital classrooms, and a strong culture of respect make us a top choice for students and parents. ...

We believe in nurturing all facets of a child’s growth: academic, social, emotional, and creative. Our experienced faculty is dedicated to encouraging curiosity, collaboration, and resilience while ensuring every learner receives personalized attention. The school infrastructure boasts a modern library, sprawling sports grounds, dedicated STEM and innovation labs, as well as arts and music studios to create a well-rounded learning environment.

Shivam Public School’s achievements stand as a testimony to our commitment: our alumni excel in leading universities, our teams win national competitions in science, technology, debate and sports, and our community outreach programs inspire responsibility and empathy. Parent engagement is at the forefront—families are active partners in every step of the journey.

We invite you to join a tradition of excellence. Discover a school where dreams take flight, individuality is celebrated, and the leaders of tomorrow are shaped.
